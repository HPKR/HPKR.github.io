My name is Hardeep Kaur. I am a holder of Post Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics for Business from St. Clair College, Windsor, Ontario. I am passionate about exploration of the data and what we can gather from the data. In my opinion, data has the power to change the world.

I started my professional journey as an Associate Software Engineer at Tech Mahindra, India. With time, I rose to senior positions and entered the world of Analytics. I have worked on multiple large and diverse datasets and explored them to draw insights out of them. My skills include data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning. I am proficient in problem solving, and enjoy working in challenging conditions. I often explore datasets posted on Kaggle, UCI Machine Learning repository and build machine learning models for data science projects. I also enjoy solving challenges posted on HackerRank.
